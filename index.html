Who We Are

Australia's Trusted FOAMICO Partner

Jensen Design & Service is an Australian-owned enterprise and FOAMICO's preferred distributor for Australia and New Zealand.

Our History

With over 40 years in the food industry, Jensen Design & Service has been providing cleaning equipment and pipe installation services since 2002. We became an established FOAMICO distributor in 2009, bringing world-class cleaning solutions to the Australian and New Zealand markets.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive solutions including design, supply, installation, and on-site consultation. Our team ensures every FOAMICO product fits properly within your workspace for optimal performance and food safety compliance.

About FOAMICO

FOAMICO is an independent manufacturer of superior cleaning solutions, operating across 50+ countries on five continents. With over 50 years of combined expertise, they develop reliable low-pressure cleaning equipment focused on food safety and cost efficiency.
